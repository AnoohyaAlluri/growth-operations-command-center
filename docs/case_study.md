# Portfolio Case Study

## Business Problem

Project updates, KPIs, blockers, decisions, and evidence are often distributed across separate systems. This limits leadership visibility and increases manual reporting effort.

## Role

Designed a public-safe operating model and analytics pipeline that converts synthetic project registers into validated KPI, risk, and executive-reporting outputs.

## Actions

- Defined a relational operating model for projects, KPIs, blockers, decisions, and updates.
- Created synthetic datasets representing cross-functional marketing and operations work.
- Built validation rules for IDs, dates, statuses, required fields, and referential integrity.
- Developed KPI attainment and project-health calculations.
- Created a risk register and leadership-ready summary generator.
- Built an interactive Streamlit dashboard.
- Added automated tests and public-safe documentation.

## Tools

Python, Pandas, Streamlit, Plotly, Pytest, CSV, Markdown, GitHub.

## Result

The repository produces reproducible synthetic outputs that demonstrate project governance, KPI accountability, risk escalation, and executive decision support.

## Portfolio Positioning

This case study supports applications for Growth Operations, Marketing Operations, Marketing Analytics, Business Analytics, Marketing Technology, and AI Operations roles.
